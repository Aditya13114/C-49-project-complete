Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborders
  • Changing color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most of them have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting facts about Camel

  • There are two types of Camel namely One hump Camel and Two hump camel
  • They have thick lips which let them forage thorny plants that other animals can't eat
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms
